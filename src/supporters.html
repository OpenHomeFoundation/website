Who funds our work

The work of the Open Home Foundation is made possible through the financial contributions of these organizations.

Commercial Partners

{% include 'svg/arrow_outward.svg' %}

Nabu Casa

Nabu Casa provides ongoing funding for the Foundation's work through the revenue generated from the sale of official Home Assistant hardware and Home Assistant Cloud subscriptions.

Commercial Partner
{% include 'svg/arrow_outward.svg' %}

Apollo Automation

Apollo Automation provides ongoing funding to the Open Home Foundation through revenue generated from the sale of advanced smart home hardware, and DIY kits and tools. 

Commercial Partner

Donors

{% include 'svg/arrow_outward.svg' %}

DuckDuckGo

DuckDuckGo, an independent Internet privacy company, shares our mission to bring privacy to the smart home and has donated to help the Foundation continue its work.

Donor Gold
{% include 'svg/arrow_outward.svg' %}

Espressif

Espressif designs many of the chips that power ESPHome. Its donation to the Open Home Foundation supports software development and the DIY sensor and controller ecosystems.

Donor Silver

We look forward to welcoming new donors as we continue to actively diversify our funding base with more organizations that believe in our mission. If you’re interested in contributing to the future of the Open Home, take a look at our Donation Tiers.
