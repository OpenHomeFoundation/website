Who supports our work

The work of the Open Home Foundation is made possible through the contributions of these organizations:

{% include 'svg/arrow_outward.svg' %}

Nabu Casa

Nabu Casa provides ongoing funding for the Foundation's work through the revenue generated from the sale of official Home Assistant hardware and Home Assistant Cloud subscriptions.

Commercial Partner
{% include 'svg/arrow_outward.svg' %}

DuckDuckGo

DuckDuckGo, an independent Internet privacy company, shares our mission to bring privacy to the smart home and has donated to help the Foundation continue its work.

Donor
{% include 'svg/arrow_outward.svg' %}

Espressif

Espressif designs many of the chips that power ESPHome. They are supporting Open Home software development to help drive forward the DIY sensor and controller revolution.
